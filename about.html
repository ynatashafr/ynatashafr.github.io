About me

Front-end developer from Valencia, Venezuela

I am a passionate Front-end Developer in training, originally from Venezuela and the Dominican Republic, and currently based in Sweden. With a newfound love for building responsive websites, I am excited to share my projects and showcase my progress in this new career path.

My decision to transition from a Data Analyst Coordinator to coding was inspired by my move from the Dominican Republic to Sweden. Witnessing the vast array of opportunities that Sweden offers in the field of technology, along with the flexibility of remote work, ignited a spark within me. As I delved into studying and learning, my fascination with programming grew exponentially. The ability to design and construct an infinite range of possibilities on the web became a driving force behind my career change.
